OUR HISTORY

"But Allah is your protector, and He is the best of helpers."

Surah Ali Imran, 3:150

  "Umar Masjid, established in 2011 EC (2018 GC) in Bole Arabsa, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, was founded as a place for worship, education, and community development. From the start, it aimed to be a welcoming space where Muslims could grow spiritually, learn, and contribute to society. Over the years, Umar Masjid has become a cornerstone for the local Muslim community, offering prayer services, Islamic education, and social programs. It continues to foster faith, unity, and service, reflecting its mission to uplift the Ummah and serve as a guiding light for all."

  Named after Umar IBN Al-Khattab (RA), a companion of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), the masjid symbolizes strength, justice, and faith. Inspired by his example, our community is committed to serving Allah and fostering justice, compassion, and service to neighbors." 

   Over the years, Umar Masjid has grown to meet community needs by improving facilities, offering Islamic education, and supporting social and charitable initiatives. We aim to create a welcoming environment where people of all ages and backgrounds can strengthen their faith and contribute to the community's well-being." 

         mission 
Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for worship, foster spiritual growth, and inspire individuals and families to live by the principles of Islam. We achieve this through education, community service, and mutual support, striving to serve Allah and uplift our Ummah. 

         Vision
Our vision is to be a guiding light for the Muslim community, promoting faith, compassion, and knowledge. We aim to foster unity, understanding, and service to humanity, creating an environment where everyone can thrive spiritually and socially.  

Our pledge 

At Umar Masjid, we are committed to transparency, integrity, and accountability. Every contribution, whether big or small, helps serve the community, complete the mosque’s construction, and support educational and social programs. Your Donations Support:

  • Maintaining and improving the mosque's facilities

  • Offering educational programs, including Quran and Hadith classes, for all age groups

  • Organizing community events, lectures, and social welfare initiatives

  • Complete the construction and furnish the mosque with necessary facilities.

CLASS AND PROGRAMS

        1. TAFSIR CLASS 

 Schedule: Fridays & Saturdays – From Maghrib to Isha
Join us for an in-depth exploration of the Quran, gaining valuable insights into its meanings and teachings. Open to all, these sessions offer an opportunity to reflect on the Quran's guidance and deepen your understanding of Islam

2 Fiqh(islamic jurisprudence) class

Schedule : Wednesday & Thursday  -From Maghrib to Isha 
   
  Joint us as we delve into the key principles of Fiqh, covering practical aspects such as prayer, fasting, zakat, and family law. This class helps you understand how to apply Islamic teachings in everyday life, deepening your knowledge of religious duties and rights. 

3.Mkhtasar al muslim (hadiths class)

Schedule: Mondays &Tuesday
From maghrib to Isha
Study essential hadiths from Mukhtasar al-Muslim, covering topics of faith, morality, and personal conduct. Gain insights into the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and their relevance in shaping our daily lives and interactions. 

4. Sira (Hazel Habib Kitab) Class

 Schedule: Daily – After Fajr
    Explore the life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) through Hazel Habib Kitab. This class covers significant events, his character, struggles, and the development of Islam. Learn valuable lessons from his life to inspire and guide your faith and actions. 

5.Daily quran and islamic studies program

             Schedule: After Asr

This program is open to all ages and focuses on: Beginner’s Classes: Learn Quran reading with Qaidah Noorania, developing pronunciation and fluency. Nazer Program: Advance to fluent Quran reading and comprehension. Tajweed Classes: Master Quranic recitation with proper pronunciation and melody. Hifz Quran & Kitab Study: Memorize the Quran and study foundational Islamic texts to nurture spiritual and intellectual growth
